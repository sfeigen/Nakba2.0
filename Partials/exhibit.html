The Exhibit

Thank you for helping to fund the first Nakba Museum exhibit in the United States. We sincerely appreciate you for believing in our project and for your contributions. We are proud to announce that we have well surpassed our $14,000 goal.

While this project is still in its early stages, we are encouraged by your enormous support. We intend to show what the Nakba Museum of Memory and Hope can offer and create a groundswell of support for a permanent space in the Washington DC area.

The Nakba Museum Inaugural Exhibit: June 12th - 27th, 2015

We have secured the venue, the art work and photography, booked the Palestinian artist to fly to Washington, D.C., and we are working to plan the two weeks of events that accompany the exhibit. We will keep you updated through our Facebook page, our website, and email updates.

Please save the date and come to see what your generosity has made possible! We will post the exhibit schedule shortly.

Our campaign is closed but you can still donate to create a permanent space for the museum.

We would like thank everyone who donated, shared, and spread the word about our campaign. And since we value each one of you, we are acknowledging your name and contribution.
